# Delta Spec

## Change ID
<!-- Example: 001-login-null-pointer -->

## Type
bugfix | feature | refactor-safe | hotfix

## Context
Describe the current flow and where the change appears.

## Current Behavior
What happens today.

## Expected Behavior
What should happen.

## Evidence
Logs, stack traces, screenshots, endpoints, files, or failing tests.

## Root Cause Hypothesis
What is most likely causing the issue.

## Impacted Areas
Modules, services, screens, APIs, jobs, database tables, or contracts.

## Constraints
Compatibility, performance, security, rollout, timeline, compliance.

## Minimal Safe Delta
Describe the smallest safe change that solves the problem.

## Alternatives Considered
List options that were rejected and why.

## Acceptance Criteria
Objective conditions for success.

## Verification Strategy
How the change will be validated.
